About Me

Hi, my name is Juan, and I am a software developer with 2 years of experience in Unity, both in 2D and 3D, as well as proficiency in C#. I have been actively involved in various personal projects, including game jams and indie game development for platforms like itch.io. I have even published a game in early access on Steam, which I worked on during my free time. Currently, I am also in the process of working on AR projects to improve in this area.

Furthermore, I have a strong background in JavaScript, encompassing both back-end technologies like Node.js and Express, as well as front-end development using React. In addition to my web development skills, I also possess experience in mobile app development using React Native and Expo.

My primary goal is to join a dynamic work team where I can apply my knowledge and skills to contribute effectively while continuously improving as a developer.
